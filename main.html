Frequently Asked Questions

How are my confidential data managed?

Your sensitive data (reports, documents, payments, history) are stored on servers located in France.

Video conferences are encrypted end-to-end, without recording or access possible by Visio‑Experts.

Payments are managed by Stripe Connect, a PCI DSS certified actor and GDPR compliant via Stripe Payments Europe.

What are the pricing plans?

We offer flexible pricing plans starting from €29/month for individual professionals, with custom enterprise solutions available. All plans include unlimited video calls, payment processing, and customer support.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start your trial.

How does the payment integration work?

Our payment system is fully integrated with Stripe. You can send payment requests before, during, or after video calls. Clients receive a secure link and can pay instantly with their credit card.

Can I use this on mobile devices?

Absolutely! Visio-Experts works seamlessly on all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile. No app download required, everything works in your browser.

What kind of support do you provide?

We provide comprehensive support via email and chat, Monday to Friday from 8 AM to 6 PM. Premium users get priority support and phone assistance.
